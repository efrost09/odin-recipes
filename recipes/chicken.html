Fried Chicken

A crispy dish full of carbs that's sure to clog your arteries, but it is SO worth it.

Ingredients

  • 30 saltine crackers
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons dry potato flakes
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 egg
  • 6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 2 cups vegetable oil

Steps

  1. put crackers in bag. Crush, add flour, potato flakes, salt and pepper. Mix.
  2. beat egg in shallow dish or bowl. Dredge chicken in egg. Place in bag with crumbs.
  3. heat oil in deep fryer to 350 degrees (175 degrees C).
  4. fry chicken. turn frequently until golden brown. 15-20 minutes.
