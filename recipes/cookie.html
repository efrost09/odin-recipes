Chocolate Chip Cookies

The best type of cookie. So good.

ingredients

  • butter
  • eggs
  • Vanilla
  • baking soda
  • water
  • salt
  • flour
  • chocolate chips

Steps

  1. beat butter, sugars, eggs, and vanilla
  2. dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to mixture
  3. stir the flour
  4. drop dough onto baking sheet
  5. bake until edges are golden brown
